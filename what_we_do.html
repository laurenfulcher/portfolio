WHAT WE DO

Our purpose is to make disciples who make disciples. We work with fellow student athletes across the country to be spreaders of God's good news.

PURPOSE

We meet monthly as a whole organization above the Natatorium. These meetings are where we hear from different speakers and join in learning more about faith.

MEETINGS

These are small Discipleship communities of student athletes. Each semester, D-groups read through a book of the Bible, and work to apply the messages learned.

DGROUPS

We partner with an organization in Detroit called Mack Development. We go to detroit to help transform run down neighborhoods into community places.

VOLUNTEERING

Host multiple community engagement events throughout the year, including game nights at Ferry Field, bowling, group activities, and more.

BONDING

At this camp, student-athletes are pushed to their physical limits and challenged to realize their spiritual limits in return, then to bring that to their sport.

CAMPS

Every year, we host an event for all student-athletes to package up christmas presents for children in situations that do not allow for gifts to be funded.

OCC

Through each of these events, our main goal is to spread the good news of God, and to encourage others to do the same. We do all things around the presence of others.

COMMUNITY

"Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of The Father and of The Son and of The Holy Spirit." Matthew 28:19
